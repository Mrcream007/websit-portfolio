Salesforce Data Migration and Cleanup

  • Data mapping and transformation using Data Loader.
  • Validation rules and duplicate management to ensure data integrity.
  • Custom reports to monitor data quality and completeness.
  • Scheduled data backups and automated data import processes.

    • Description:

    I successfully led a comprehensive data migration and cleanup project for a mid-sized company transitioning to Salesforce CRM. The project began with a thorough assessment of existing data sources, including legacy systems, spreadsheets, and third-party applications. I collaborated closely with stakeholders to understand data requirements, identify critical data elements, and establish data quality standards.

    The migration process involved extracting, transforming, and loading (ETL) data into Salesforce. I used data migration tools like Data Loader and third-party ETL solutions to ensure a seamless and accurate transfer of data. Throughout this process, I conducted rigorous data validation and cleansing to eliminate duplicates, correct inaccuracies, and standardize formats. This ensured that the migrated data was clean, reliable, and ready for use in Salesforce.

    Post-migration, I implemented data governance practices, including regular data audits, to maintain data integrity and quality. I also trained end-users on data management best practices within Salesforce, ensuring they could effectively manage and utilize the clean, migrated data. This project not only streamlined the company's data management processes but also significantly improved data accuracy, enhancing overall business decision-making capabilities.
